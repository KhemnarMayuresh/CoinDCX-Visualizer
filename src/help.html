How to Download CSV File:
  1. Login to your coindcx account using url.
  2. After login click on 'Pro'. Inside that click on 'Spot' as shown in below image.
  3. Click on 'ORDERS'. Inside that click on 'Insta History' as shown in below image.
  4. On Insta History click on Download CSV.
  5. Select duration and click on download.

How to Visualize CSV File:
  1. Open url page.
  2. Select your CSV file.
  3. After seleting file you can notice input fields with Currency names. Open your 'my Investment' as shown in below image. Enter each currency value in input field.
  4. Click on 'Generate'.

Use of Each chart:
  1. Total Table - Showing profit amount in rs for investment in each currency.
  2. Average Prices - Showing average price of buying and selling. Based on average buying price you can decide what should be minimum currency price sell.
  3. Current Invested Amount - Showing current invested amount in each currency.
  4. Buy-Sell-Hold-Profit Chart -Showing bar chart for each action (Buy,Sell,Hold,Profit).
